About Us

A2 Pyramid Edutech, specializes in teaching MERN Stack Web Development, Data Science, and Machine Learning.

Welcome to A2 Pyramid Edutech, where we're redefining education for the modern world. We specialize in MERN Stack Web Development, Data Science, and Machine Learning, offering a unique model that ensures you don’t pay until you secure a job. Our mission is to equip you with the skills and resources you need to excel in your career.

Traditional higher education often leaves many behind, resulting in hefty debts and uncertain job prospects. At A2 Pyramid Edutech, we believe there’s a better path forward. We are committed to providing an affordable, risk-free route to a successful career.

In addition to our educational programs, we also offer practical experience through real-world projects. We take on web development projects, creating and maintaining websites, as well as data science and machine learning projects. Our students gain hands-on experience that makes them stand out in the job market.

Furthermore, we offer internships to provide you with valuable industry experience and support you in securing placement opportunities. At A2 Pyramid Edutech, we bet on our learners’ potential and are ready to bet on you.

Join us to accelerate your career, gain practical experience, and achieve your professional goals—without the financial burden.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for our courses, you must meet the following requirements:

  • B.Tech (All Streams) with an aggregate of above 60% (including 10th, 12th, and BTech)
  • BCA graduates or final year students with a minimum of 60% (including 10th, 12th, and BCA)
  • MCA graduates with a minimum of 60% (including 10th, 12th, and MCA)

Meet these qualifications? You're eligible to apply for our training programs and benefit from our job placement assistance.

Note: If you are currently pursuing your degree and expect to meet these criteria upon graduation, you may also apply. Ensure that your final marks meet the required percentages.
